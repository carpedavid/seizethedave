---
title: "the cows all fly"
date: "2011-01-31"
categories: 
  - "poetry"
tags: 
  - "childrens-poetry"
---

The cows all fly on aeroplanes  
Beneath the bright blue sky  
Up there amongst the wind and clouds  
Up there, up oh-so high
